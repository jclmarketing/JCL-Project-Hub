Complete Locksmith Services

Emergency Locksmith Services

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your home, office or car? We use non-destructive entry methods to get you back inside fast.

Break-In Repairs

Been burgled? We provide emergency boarding up, lock replacement and full security upgrades on the spot.

Broken Key Extraction

Snapped key stuck in the lock? We'll extract it without damaging your lock and cut a new key on site.

24/7 Rapid Response

Day or night, weekday or weekend. Same price, no call-out fee, 30-minute average response time.

Residential Locksmith Services

Protecting your home with the latest lock technology and expert installation.

Lock Changes & Upgrades

Moving home or lost your keys? We supply and fit all types of residential locks including BS3621 insurance-approved.

uPVC & Composite Doors

Specialist repair of uPVC mechanisms, gearboxes, euro cylinders and multi-point locking systems.

Window & Patio Locks

Secure all entry points with quality window locks, patio door locks and storm door lock installations.

Anti-Snap Cylinders

Upgrade to anti-snap, anti-bump, anti-pick cylinders. The UK's most popular security upgrade.

Garage & Gate Locks

Complete locking solutions for garages, sheds, gates and outbuildings to keep your property secure.

Key Cutting & Rekeying

On-site key cutting for all lock types. Rekeying service so your existing locks work with new keys.

Commercial Locksmith Services

Tailored security solutions for businesses of all sizes across Glasgow.

Master Key Systems

Design and install master key suites giving controlled access across your entire premises with a single key.

Access Control

Electronic keypads, card readers and digital access control systems for modern commercial security.

Shop Front Security

Specialist locks and repairs for shop front doors, glass doors, steel shutters and roller doors.

Safe Opening & Supply

Professional safe opening, combination changes and supply of new safes for cash and valuables.

Door Closers & Hardware

Supply and fit commercial-grade door closers, panic bars, push pads and fire door hardware.

Business Accounts

Dedicated account management for letting agents, housing associations and property managers.

Auto Locksmith Services

Specialist vehicle locksmith for all makes and models. Roadside assistance available.

Car Lockout

Locked out of your vehicle? Our auto specialists use non-destructive methods to get you back on the road.

Car Key Replacement

Lost or damaged car keys? We cut and programme replacement keys for most makes and models on site.

Transponder Programming

Key fob programming and transponder chip coding for modern vehicles with electronic immobilisers.

Van & Commercial Vehicles

Security solutions for vans, trucks and fleet vehicles including specialist van locks and deadlocks.

Lock Upgrades & Security

Upgrade your existing locks to the latest high-security standards and keep intruders out.

Smart Lock Installation

WiFi and Bluetooth smart lock fitting. Keyless entry, remote locking from your phone, and audit trails for your property.

British Standard Locks

Upgrade to BS3621 insurance-approved locks. Required by most home insurers and the gold standard for door security.

High-Security Cylinders

Anti-snap, anti-bump, anti-pick and anti-drill euro cylinders. Protect against the most common break-in methods.

Multi-Point Lock Upgrades

Upgrade your uPVC or composite door to a modern multi-point locking mechanism for maximum security at every bolt point.

Restricted Key Systems

Patented key profiles that cannot be copied without authorisation. Ideal for landlords and shared properties.
