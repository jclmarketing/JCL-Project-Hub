FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you get to me?

We aim to reach you within 30 minutes of your call. In most cases across Glasgow and Clydebank, we're there even faster. We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Is there a callout charge?

No. We never charge a callout fee. You only pay for the work carried out and any parts required. We'll always give you a clear price before starting any work.

Are you insured?

Yes, we are fully insured and DBS checked. We're also verified on Checkatrade and Rated People, so you can book with complete confidence.

Can you open my door without damaging the lock?

In the vast majority of cases, yes. We use non-destructive entry techniques wherever possible. If a lock does need to be replaced, we'll always discuss this with you first and provide a quote before proceeding.

Do you supply locks or should I buy my own?

We supply and fit all types of locks from trusted brands like Yale, ERA, Ultion, and more. All locks come with a 12-month guarantee. We carry a range of locks in our van so most jobs are completed in a single visit.

Will my insurance accept your locks?

Yes. We supply BS3621 insurance-approved locks as standard for all lock changes. These meet the requirements of all major UK insurance providers.
