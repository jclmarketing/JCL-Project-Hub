The Benefits of LED Lighting

LED technology has revolutionised the lighting industry, and upgrading to LED is one of the most impactful improvements you can make to your property. Here are the key benefits:

Energy Savings

LED bulbs use up to 80% less energy than traditional halogen or incandescent lamps. This translates to significant savings on your electricity bills, with most homeowners seeing a noticeable reduction within the first month of switching.

Exceptional Longevity

A quality LED lamp can last up to 50,000 hours, compared to just 2,000 hours for a traditional halogen bulb. This means fewer replacements, less maintenance and lower long-term costs for your home or business.

Superior Light Quality

Modern LEDs are available in a wide range of colour temperatures, from warm white to cool daylight, with excellent colour rendering. They provide instant full brightness with no warm-up time and are available in dimmable versions for complete control over your ambiance.

Residential Lighting Design

We work closely with homeowners to design lighting schemes that complement their interiors and lifestyle. From layered lighting in kitchens and living areas, using a combination of downlights, pendants and under-cabinet strips, to relaxing bathroom lighting and atmospheric bedroom setups, we create spaces that look and feel exactly right.

Commercial & Emergency Lighting

For commercial clients, we design and install lighting systems that meet workplace regulations, enhance productivity and create inviting environments for customers and staff. We also install emergency lighting systems to BS 5266 standards, including maintained and non-maintained fittings, exit signs and central battery systems. Regular testing and maintenance packages are available to ensure ongoing compliance.
