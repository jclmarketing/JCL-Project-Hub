Commercial Electrical Services

Comprehensive electrical solutions for businesses, offices and commercial properties. We deliver professional installations, maintenance and compliance services that keep your business running safely and efficiently.

Powering Your Business

Averis Electrical Solutions provides a full range of commercial electrical services tailored to the unique demands of business environments. We understand that downtime costs money, which is why we work efficiently and flexibly around your operating hours to minimise disruption.

From new office fit-outs and retail lighting schemes to ongoing maintenance contracts and compliance testing, we have the experience and expertise to handle projects of any scale. Our commercial team is experienced in working across a wide range of business sectors, delivering solutions that are safe, compliant and designed to support your operations.

Key Commercial Services

Our commercial electrical services cover every aspect of your business electrical needs.

Office Fit-Outs

Complete electrical installations for new offices and refurbishments, including power, lighting, data and communications infrastructure.

  • Power distribution and layout
  • Desk power and floor boxes
  • Containment and trunking systems
  • Meeting room AV wiring

Retail & Commercial Lighting

Energy-efficient lighting design and installation that enhances your commercial space and reduces running costs.

  • LED lighting upgrades
  • Display and accent lighting
  • Automated lighting controls
  • Energy-saving solutions

Data Cabling

Structured data cabling installations for reliable network connectivity throughout your commercial premises.

  • Cat5e, Cat6 and Cat6a cabling
  • Fibre optic installations
  • Server room wiring
  • Patch panel and cabinet setup

Three-Phase Installations

Three-phase power supply installations and upgrades for commercial properties with higher power demands.

  • Three-phase supply upgrades
  • Distribution board installations
  • Load balancing and assessment
  • Power factor correction

Fire Alarm Systems

Design, installation, commissioning and maintenance of fire alarm systems to meet your building's requirements.

  • Conventional and addressable systems
  • System design to BS 5839
  • Installation and commissioning
  • Ongoing maintenance contracts

Emergency Lighting

Compliant emergency lighting installations to ensure safe evacuation routes and meet building regulations.

  • Escape route lighting
  • Open area and high-risk lighting
  • Self-test and central battery systems
  • Annual testing and certification

Sectors We Serve

We have extensive experience working across a diverse range of commercial sectors, understanding the specific requirements and regulations of each.

Offices

From small offices to large corporate headquarters, we deliver complete electrical solutions including power distribution, lighting, data cabling and energy management systems. We work around your business hours to avoid disruption to your operations.

Retail

Eye-catching lighting, reliable power for point-of-sale systems and security installations for shops, showrooms and retail units. We understand the importance of presentation and can design lighting schemes that showcase your products at their best.

Restaurants & Hospitality

Specialist electrical services for restaurants, pubs, hotels and leisure venues. From commercial kitchen wiring and extraction systems to ambient dining area lighting and outdoor terrace power supplies.

Healthcare

Reliable and compliant electrical installations for clinics, dental practices, care homes and medical facilities. We understand the critical nature of power in healthcare settings and the stringent compliance requirements involved.

Education

Electrical services for schools, colleges and training centres. From classroom lighting and interactive whiteboard power supplies to laboratory installations and sports facility lighting, we deliver safe and compliant solutions for learning environments.
