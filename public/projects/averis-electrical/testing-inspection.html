Electrical Testing & Inspection

Comprehensive electrical testing and inspection services for all types of properties. We test to the latest IET Wiring Regulations and provide detailed reports.

Professional Electrical Testing Services

Regular electrical testing and inspection is essential for ensuring the safety of any property. Whether you are a homeowner, landlord, business owner, or facilities manager, having your electrical systems professionally tested helps identify potential hazards before they become dangerous, ensures compliance with regulations, and gives you documented proof that your installation is safe.

At Averis Electrical Solutions, we provide a full range of electrical testing and inspection services carried out by qualified and experienced engineers. All testing is performed in accordance with the latest IET Wiring Regulations (BS 7671) and we provide clear, detailed reports with actionable recommendations.

Our Testing & Inspection Services Include

  • Fixed wire testing
  • Portable appliance testing (PAT)
  • Emergency lighting testing
  • Fire alarm testing
  • Earth loop impedance testing
  • Insulation resistance testing
  • RCD testing
  • Thermal imaging surveys

Fixed Wire Testing

Fixed wire testing, also known as periodic inspection, involves a thorough examination and testing of the permanent electrical installation within a property. This includes the wiring, distribution boards, protective devices, and earthing arrangements. The results are documented in an Electrical Installation Condition Report (EICR), which details the overall condition of the installation and highlights any defects or areas of concern.

PAT Testing

Portable Appliance Testing (PAT) is the examination of electrical appliances and equipment to ensure they are safe to use. This is particularly important for commercial and industrial environments where employers have a duty of care to ensure all electrical equipment is safe. We provide efficient PAT testing services with minimal disruption to your business operations, complete with labels and certification.

Specialist Testing Services

Beyond standard fixed wire and PAT testing, we also offer specialist testing services including emergency lighting testing, fire alarm testing, thermal imaging surveys, and bespoke testing packages for commercial and industrial clients. Thermal imaging surveys are particularly valuable for identifying hot spots in electrical systems that could indicate loose connections, overloaded circuits, or failing components before they cause a fault or fire.

All testing is carried out by our NICEIC-approved engineers using calibrated instruments, and every report is produced to the highest professional standards.
