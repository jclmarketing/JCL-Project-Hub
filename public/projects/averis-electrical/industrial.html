Industrial Electrical Services

Specialist electrical solutions for industrial facilities, factories and heavy-duty environments. Our experienced industrial electricians deliver safe, robust and compliant installations that keep your operations running at full capacity.

Industrial Electrical Expertise

Averis Electrical Solutions brings specialist knowledge and hands-on experience to every industrial electrical project. We understand the demanding nature of industrial environments and the critical importance of reliable, safe power systems to your operations.

Our industrial team is experienced in working within live production environments, during planned shutdowns and on new-build projects. We work closely with facilities managers, engineers and project managers to deliver solutions that are robust, compliant and designed for the long term. From heavy power installations and machinery connections to complex containment systems and hazardous area work, we have the skills and accreditations to handle the most challenging industrial electrical projects.

Key Industrial Services

Comprehensive electrical services designed for the demands of industrial facilities and operations.

Heavy Power Installations

High-capacity power installations for industrial facilities requiring substantial electrical supply to support heavy plant and equipment.

  • High-voltage supply connections
  • Transformer installations
  • Busbar trunking systems
  • Power factor correction

Machinery Wiring & Connections

Professional wiring, connection and commissioning of industrial machinery and production equipment.

  • Machine supply and isolation
  • Production line wiring
  • Conveyor system electrics
  • Commissioning and testing

Containment Systems

Design and installation of cable management and containment systems for industrial environments.

  • Cable tray and ladder rack
  • Steel wire basket systems
  • Conduit and trunking
  • Fire-rated containment

Motor Control & Drives

Installation and configuration of motor control centres, variable speed drives and soft starters for industrial motors.

  • Motor control centres (MCC)
  • Variable frequency drives (VFD)
  • Soft starter installations
  • Star-delta and DOL starters

Distribution Boards & Panels

Design, build, installation and modification of electrical distribution boards and control panels for industrial applications.

  • Main switchboards
  • Sub-distribution boards
  • Control panel design and build
  • Panel modifications and upgrades

Hazardous Area Work

Specialist electrical installations in hazardous areas classified under ATEX and DSEAR regulations.

  • ATEX-compliant installations
  • Zone classification expertise
  • Intrinsically safe systems
  • Hazardous area inspection and testing

Industries We Serve

We work across a wide range of industrial sectors, bringing specialist knowledge and experience to every project.

Manufacturing

Electrical installations and maintenance for manufacturing plants, including production line wiring, machine connections, control systems and power distribution. We understand the demands of continuous production environments and the importance of minimising downtime during electrical work.

Warehousing & Distribution

Complete electrical solutions for warehouses and distribution centres, from high-bay lighting and loading dock power supplies to automated system wiring and EV charging for fleet vehicles. We deliver efficient lighting and power schemes for large-scale facilities.

Food Processing

Specialist electrical work for food processing facilities, meeting the stringent hygiene and safety standards required. We install IP-rated equipment, stainless steel containment, wash-down resistant fittings and temperature monitoring systems for cold stores and production areas.

Engineering & Heavy Industry

Robust electrical installations for engineering workshops, fabrication facilities and heavy industrial sites. From overhead crane supplies and welding station power to compressed air system wiring and heavy machine connections, we handle the most demanding industrial environments.
