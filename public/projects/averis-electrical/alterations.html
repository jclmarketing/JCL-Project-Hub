Electrical Alterations

All types of electrical alterations and additions for homes and businesses. Whether you need extra sockets, relocated switches or circuit modifications, we ensure everything is safe and compliant.

Safe & Compliant Electrical Alterations

Whether you are renovating your home, reconfiguring an office layout, or simply need additional power points, our qualified electricians carry out all types of electrical alterations and additions. Every modification we make is fully tested, certified and compliant with the latest BS 7671 wiring regulations.

Electrical alterations may seem straightforward, but they must be carried out by a competent person to ensure safety. Incorrectly installed wiring, overloaded circuits and non-compliant modifications can create serious fire and shock risks. At Averis Electrical Solutions, we take the time to assess your existing installation, plan the work carefully and deliver a neat, professional finish every time.

Domestic Electrical Alterations

Homes evolve over time, and your electrical installation needs to keep pace with how you use your space. Whether you are converting a garage, adding a home office, upgrading your kitchen or simply need more sockets in the living room, we can help. We also install USB charging sockets, dimmer switches and smart home controls to modernise your property and improve convenience.

Commercial Electrical Alterations

Office relocations, shop refits and workspace reconfigurations all require electrical alterations. We work with businesses of all sizes to ensure your electrical infrastructure supports your operational needs. From adding data points and power outlets to reconfiguring lighting circuits and installing dedicated supplies for new equipment, our team delivers minimal disruption and maximum reliability.

Safety & Compliance

All electrical alterations we carry out are notifiable under Part P of the Building Regulations. This means the work must be carried out by a registered competent person or inspected by your local Building Control body. As an accredited contractor, we self-certify all our work and provide you with the appropriate electrical certificates, giving you complete peace of mind that the job has been done safely and to the required standard.
