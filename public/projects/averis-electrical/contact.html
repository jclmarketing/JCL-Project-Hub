Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question about getting in touch or requesting a quote? Here are answers to some of the most common queries we receive.

How do I get a free quote?

Simply fill in the contact form above, give us a call on 01234 567 890 or email us at info@averiselectricalsolutions.co.uk. We'll arrange a convenient time to visit your property, assess the work required and provide a detailed written quote at no cost.

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours during working days. If your enquiry is urgent, we recommend calling us directly for the fastest response.

Do you offer emergency call-outs?

Yes, we provide a 24/7 emergency call-out service. If you have an electrical emergency such as a power failure, burning smell, exposed wiring or tripping circuits, call us immediately on 01234 567 890 and we'll respond as quickly as possible.

Is there a charge for a quote?

No, all our quotes are completely free with no obligation. We believe in transparent pricing and will never pressure you into booking work.

What areas do you cover?

We cover Birmingham, Solihull, Wolverhampton, Dudley, Walsall, Sutton Coldfield, Tamworth, Coventry and the wider West Midlands region. Visit our Areas We Cover page for the full list.

What information should I include in my enquiry?

The more detail you can provide, the better. Let us know the type of work you need, the property type (domestic, commercial or industrial), any specific requirements or timescales, and the best way to contact you. This helps us provide the most accurate quote possible.

Prefer to Speak to Someone?

Our friendly team is available to take your call during working hours. For emergencies, we're available 24/7.
