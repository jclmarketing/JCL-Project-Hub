New Electrical Installations

Complete electrical installations for new builds, extensions, loft conversions, and renovations. First fix, second fix, and full fit-out services fully compliant with BS 7671.

Professional Electrical Installations You Can Rely On

Whether you are building a new home, extending your current property, converting a loft, or renovating a commercial space, Averis Electrical Solutions provides comprehensive electrical installation services tailored to your project's needs.

Our team of qualified electricians handles every stage of the installation process, from first fix wiring through to second fix finishing and final testing. Every installation is carried out to the highest standards and fully complies with the current BS 7671 Wiring Regulations (18th Edition).

Our New Installation Services Include

  • New build wiring
  • Extension electrics
  • Loft conversion wiring
  • Kitchen/bathroom electrics
  • Garden room/outbuilding electrics
  • Socket and switch installations
  • Distribution board installation
  • Outdoor power and lighting

First Fix & Second Fix Electrical Work

First fix involves running cables, installing back boxes, and laying the groundwork before plastering and finishing takes place. Second fix is when sockets, switches, light fittings, and the consumer unit are connected and made live. We manage both stages seamlessly, working closely with other trades to keep your project on schedule.

Why Choose Averis for New Installations?

We have over 15 years of experience delivering electrical installations for domestic, commercial, and industrial projects. Every installation is planned meticulously, carried out by fully qualified electricians, and completed with full testing and certification. We are NICEIC approved, giving you complete peace of mind that the work meets the highest industry standards.

We also liaise directly with building control and other trades on your behalf, making the process as smooth and stress-free as possible for you.
