FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a renovation or extension cost?

Costs vary depending on scope, site conditions, and specification level. As a guide, quality renovations and extensions in Melbourne typically range from $3,500 to $5,000+ per square metre. We provide a detailed, fixed-price quote after our initial consultation and design review.

Do you handle the design or just the build?

We are a construction firm, not architects. However, we work closely with architects and can recommend trusted design partners. We offer buildability reviews early in the design stage to ensure plans are practical, cost-effective, and achievable.

How long does a typical build take?

A standard renovation or extension typically takes 6-9 months from start to completion. Larger or more complex projects may take longer. We provide a detailed timeline during the quoting process and keep you updated throughout.

Are you a registered builder?

Yes. Adam Heat holds a Victorian Building Authority (VBA) registration and a Certificate IV in Building & Construction from Holmesglen. We carry full contract works insurance and public liability insurance.

Will you be on site during the build?

Absolutely. Adam personally oversees every project and is on site regularly. You'll have direct communication with him throughout — no call centres or project managers as middlemen.

Do you offer a structural guarantee?

Yes. All our work comes with a structural guarantee in accordance with Victorian building regulations. We stand behind the quality of our builds long after handover.
