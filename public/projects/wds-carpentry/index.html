FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you cover?

We're based in Herne Bay and cover all of South East Kent, including Whitstable, Canterbury, Margate, Broadstairs, Ramsgate, Faversham, and surrounding villages.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, all our quotes are free and come with no obligation. We'll visit your property, discuss your requirements, and provide a detailed written quote so you know exactly what to expect.

Are you fully insured?

Yes, we carry full public liability insurance. We're happy to provide proof of insurance on request.

What type of work do you do?

We offer a full range of carpentry and general building services including extensions, loft conversions, kitchens, bathrooms, roofing, fascias, decking, fencing, plastering, brickwork, and all types of carpentry and joinery work.

How long will my project take?

Timescales depend on the size and complexity of the project. We always provide an estimated timeline with your quote and keep you updated throughout. We pride ourselves on sticking to agreed schedules.

Do you handle all trades or just carpentry?

While carpentry is our core trade, we provide full general building services. We have a trusted network of qualified tradespeople including plumbers, electricians, and plasterers who work alongside us on larger projects.
