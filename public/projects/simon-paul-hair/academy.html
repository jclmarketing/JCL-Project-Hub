Simon Paul Hair & Beauty is currently the only VTCT endorsed satellite centre to Solihull College — a distinction that reflects our commitment to delivering industry-leading training within a real salon environment.

Our Track Record

Key Achievements

Over 75 young stylists have entered the industry through the Simon Paul Hair Academy programme, many going on to build outstanding careers in hairdressing.

Several of our apprentices have gone on to become salon owners in their own right, demonstrating the strength of the foundation our training provides. The programme has also produced Student of the Year Award winners, showcasing the exceptional talent nurtured within our academy.

One apprentice advanced through the ranks to become a senior team member at Simon Paul Hair & Beauty — a testament to the career progression opportunities available to those who train with us.

How It Works

Training Structure

Every Monday is dedicated to bespoke in-salon training, delivered in a nearly one-to-one environment to ensure each apprentice receives the focused attention they need to develop their skills.

On the remaining weekdays, apprentices work alongside the styling team, gaining hands-on experience in a busy, professional salon. Training is carried out using real models and is held to full industry standards, so graduates are fully prepared for a career in hairdressing from day one.
