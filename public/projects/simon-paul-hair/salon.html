About Simon Paul Hair & Beauty

Simon Paul Hair & Beauty provides a contemporary, relaxed environment all built around giving the client a total experience. Every first-time visitor receives a complimentary consultation with our stylists and colour technicians. We offer an individual, bespoke service.

Located in the heart of Knowle village, near Solihull high street, the salon is easily accessible from central Solihull and the M42 motorway. Free parking is available for all clients.

2016 saw Simon Paul Hair & Beauty celebrate 25 Years in the industry — a milestone that reflects our enduring commitment to excellence and client satisfaction.
