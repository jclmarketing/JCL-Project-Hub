Expert Barbering by Ed

Ed is a fully qualified barber who takes the time to assess each client's hair type and facial shape to deliver the perfect cut. His services include precision haircuts, beard trimming, and traditional wet shaves – all tailored to suit you.

We understand that maintenance is important, so Ed always considers how easy it will be for clients to maintain their look between appointments. Whether you're after a classic cut or something more contemporary, you're in expert hands.
