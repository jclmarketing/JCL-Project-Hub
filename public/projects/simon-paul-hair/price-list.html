Additional Information

By Quotation Only

Balayage, Colour Correction, Wedding Hair, Permanent Straightening and Extensions are all available by quotation only. Please book a complimentary consultation to discuss your requirements.

Children Under 14

Children under 14 receive discounted rates on selected services. Please note that children's discounts are excluded after 5pm on weekdays and all day on Saturdays.

Allergy Test Required

A 48-hour allergy test is required for all new technical (colour) clients prior to their first appointment. Please contact the salon to arrange your test.

Cancellation Policy

We kindly request a minimum of 24 hours' notice for any appointment cancellations or changes. This allows us to offer the slot to another client.
