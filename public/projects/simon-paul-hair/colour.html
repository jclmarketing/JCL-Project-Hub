Why Choose Us

L'Oréal Accredited Colour Specialists

At Simon Paul Hair & Beauty, colour is one of our greatest passions. As an L'Oréal accredited salon, our team undergoes continuous advanced training to stay at the forefront of colouring techniques and trends.

We use only premium products from L'Oréal Professionnel, Redken, and Kérastase to ensure your colour is vibrant, long-lasting, and kind to your hair. Every colour service begins with a thorough consultation so we can understand your vision and recommend the perfect approach.
