Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1991, Simon Paul Hair & Beauty has gained a wealth of industry experience and a prestigious training academy with a dedicated team of highly specialist staff.

Located in the centre of Knowle, Solihull, we pioneered as the first designer hair salon in the area and established ourselves through top quality, innovative hair design.

Our Journey

Through the Years

1991

Where It All Began

Simon Paul Hair & Beauty established as the first designer hair salon in Knowle, Solihull.

1994

Expanding Horizons

Expanded into premises above, incorporating hair and beauty services with contemporary yet timeless aesthetics.

2001

Academy Recognition

Hair Academy gained City & Guilds independent Centre status.

2002

Second Location

Opened second location on Drury Lane, Solihull.

2008

Third Salon Opens

Opened third location in Manor Walk, Touchwood.

2016

25 Years Strong

Celebrated 25 Years in the industry. Centralised operations focusing on flagship Knowle salon.

Present

Looking Forward

Continuing to deliver cutting edge hair design with over 75 trained stylists having entered the industry.
