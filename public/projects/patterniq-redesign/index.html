The Reality

Damp & Mould Don't Fix Themselves

Left untreated, damp and mould cause serious damage to your property and health. Recognise these warning signs?

Rising Damp

Tide marks creeping up your walls, peeling wallpaper, and salt deposits signal ground moisture rising through a failed damp proof course.

Penetrating Damp

Wet patches after rain, crumbling plaster, and damp spots on internal walls reveal rainwater breaching your external defences.

Black Mould

Dark patches spreading across walls and ceilings — more than unsightly, mould spores pose genuine health risks to your family.

Basement Flooding

Water ingress in below-ground spaces causes structural damage, renders rooms unusable, and tanks property value.

Every day you wait, the damage gets worse — and costlier to fix.
