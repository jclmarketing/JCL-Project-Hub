Efficient, low-carbon heating that extracts warmth from outside air. Slash heating costs by up to 50%.

Heat Pumps

Efficient Heating from Thin Air

Air source heat pumps (ASHPs) absorb heat from outside air and use it to warm your home and hot water. They work efficiently even in temperatures as low as -15°C, making them ideal for the UK climate.

With government incentives through the Boiler Upgrade Scheme (BUS) offering grants of up to £7,500, there has never been a better time to switch to an air source heat pump.

  • Up to 50% lower heating costs vs gas boilers
  • Eligible for £7,500 BUS grant
  • Works in temperatures down to -15°C
  • Provides heating and hot water
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Can also provide cooling in summer

How It Works

The Science Behind ASHPs

1

Absorb

The outdoor unit absorbs heat from the air, even at low temperatures, using refrigerant.

2

Compress

A compressor increases the temperature of the absorbed heat to a usable level.

3

Transfer

The heat is transferred to your central heating system and hot water cylinder.

4

Recycle

The cooled refrigerant cycles back to absorb more heat. The process repeats continuously.
