Ground Source Heat Pumps

The most efficient heat pump technology available. Harness the earth's natural warmth for reliable, low-cost heating.

GSHP

Heating from the Ground Up

Ground source heat pumps (GSHPs) extract heat from the ground using buried pipe loops. Because the ground maintains a stable temperature year-round, GSHPs deliver consistently high efficiency regardless of air temperature.

While the upfront investment is higher than air source systems, GSHPs offer the best long-term efficiency, lowest running costs, and longest system lifespan of any heat pump technology.

  • Highest efficiency of any heat pump type (COP 4-5)
  • Consistent performance year-round
  • Eligible for £7,500 BUS grant
  • Virtually silent operation
  • No outdoor unit — no visual impact
  • 50+ year ground loop lifespan

GSHP vs ASHP

🌎

Ground Source (GSHP)

Higher upfront cost but superior long-term efficiency. Best for new builds and properties with garden space. COP of 4-5 means you get 4-5 kWh of heat for every 1 kWh of electricity.

🌡

Air Source (ASHP)

Lower installation cost and easier to retrofit. Ideal for existing properties with limited space. COP of 2.5-3.5. Slight efficiency drop in very cold weather compared to GSHP.
