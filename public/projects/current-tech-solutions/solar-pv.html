Solar Energy

Harness the Power of the Sun

Solar PV systems convert sunlight into electricity for your home or business. With rising energy costs, solar panels offer a proven way to slash your electricity bills and reduce your carbon footprint.

Current Tech Solutions designs and installs bespoke solar PV systems optimised for your roof orientation, energy usage, and budget. Every installation is MCS-certified and eligible for the Smart Export Guarantee (SEG), meaning you can earn money from surplus energy.

  • Premium tier-1 solar panels
  • MCS-certified installation
  • Smart Export Guarantee eligible
  • 25-year panel performance warranty
