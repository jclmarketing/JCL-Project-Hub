Battery Storage Systems

Store excess energy and achieve energy independence. Maximise your solar investment with smart battery technology.

Energy Storage

Store Energy, Save Money

Battery storage systems let you capture excess solar energy during the day and use it in the evening when electricity rates are highest. Even without solar panels, batteries can charge from cheap off-peak tariffs and power your home during peak times.

Current Tech Solutions installs leading battery systems from trusted manufacturers, designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing electrical setup and solar PV system.

  • Compatible with new and existing solar PV
  • Charge from solar or off-peak electricity
  • Backup power during grid outages
  • Smart energy management via app
  • 10-year manufacturer warranty
  • Scalable capacity to match your needs
