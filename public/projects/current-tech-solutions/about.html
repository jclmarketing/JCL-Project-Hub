About Current Tech Solutions

A family-owned electrical services company dedicated to precision, quality, and innovation.

Our Story

Built on Experience & Trust

Current Tech Solutions was founded by Jack Guest, a skilled electrician with over 8 years of industry experience. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team serving the entire West Midlands region.

We're proud to be a family-owned business that still values personal service, honest pricing, and quality workmanship above all else. Every project, large or small, receives the same attention to detail and professional care.

  • Founded with a vision for quality electrical services
  • Grown through reputation and repeat business
  • Invested in the latest tools and training
  • Committed to sustainable energy solutions
