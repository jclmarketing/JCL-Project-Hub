House Rewiring

Full and partial rewires to keep your property safe and compliant

Professional House Rewiring in Birmingham

If your property is more than 25-30 years old and hasn't been rewired, there's a good chance the wiring is outdated and potentially unsafe. Old wiring systems can present a serious fire risk and may not cope with the electrical demands of modern life.

CP Electrics provides full and partial rewiring services across Birmingham and the West Midlands. We carry out every rewire to the latest BS 7671 regulations, ensuring your home is safe, compliant, and ready for modern living.

Signs You May Need a Rewire

  • Frequent tripping of fuses or circuit breakers
  • Burn marks or scorch marks around sockets
  • Old round-pin sockets or fabric-covered cables
  • Flickering or dimming lights
  • A smell of burning with no visible source
  • An old-style fuse box without RCD protection

Our Rewiring Process

We start with a thorough assessment of your existing wiring and discuss your requirements. We'll plan the most efficient route for new cables, minimising disruption to your home. A typical full rewire takes 5-7 days for an average three-bedroom house.

Once complete, we carry out full testing and certification, and can arrange for any making good (plastering) if required. You'll receive all the necessary electrical certificates for your records and any future property sale.

What's Included

  • New consumer unit with RCD protection
  • New wiring throughout the property
  • New sockets, switches, and light fittings
  • Full testing and certification
  • Compliance with BS 7671 regulations
