Lighting Design & Installation

Transform your space with expert lighting solutions

Professional Lighting Installation in Birmingham

Good lighting can completely transform a room. Whether you want to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere, brighten a dark space, or add kerb appeal with outdoor lighting, CP Electrics has the expertise to make it happen.

We work with you to design a lighting scheme that suits your style and budget, then handle the full installation to the highest standards.

Interior Lighting

  • Recessed downlight installation
  • LED strip and accent lighting
  • Pendant and chandelier fitting
  • Under-cabinet kitchen lighting
  • Bathroom lighting (IP-rated zones)
  • Dimmer switch installation

Outdoor Lighting

  • Garden and pathway lighting
  • Security floodlights and PIR sensors
  • Patio and decking lighting
  • Wall-mounted exterior lights

Smart Lighting

Control your lights from your phone, set schedules, and create scenes with smart lighting. We install and configure systems from leading brands, integrating with your existing smart home setup or starting from scratch.

LED Upgrades

Switching to LED can reduce your lighting energy consumption by up to 80%. We can replace your existing halogen or fluorescent fittings with modern, efficient LEDs. The quality of light is excellent, the bulbs last significantly longer, and you'll notice the savings on your energy bills straight away.
