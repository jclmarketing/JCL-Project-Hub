Consumer Unit & Fuse Board Upgrades

Your consumer unit (fuse board) is the heart of your home's electrical system. If you still have an old-style fuse box with rewirable fuses, upgrading to a modern consumer unit with RCD protection is one of the most important safety improvements you can make.

Modern consumer units contain Residual Current Devices (RCDs) that detect faults and cut the power in milliseconds, dramatically reducing the risk of electric shock and electrical fires.

Why Upgrade Your Consumer Unit?

  • Enhanced protection against electric shock
  • Reduced risk of electrical fires
  • Compliance with current regulations (18th Edition)
  • May be required by your home insurance
  • Easier identification of faulty circuits
  • Adds value when selling your property

What to Expect

A consumer unit upgrade is typically completed in a single day. We'll disconnect the old unit, install the new one, connect all existing circuits, and carry out full testing. You'll receive an electrical installation certificate and Building Regulations notification.

We use only high-quality, metal-clad consumer units from trusted brands for maximum safety and durability.
