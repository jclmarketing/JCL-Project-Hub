Home Electrical Services in Birmingham

Whether you need extra sockets, new lighting, or a complete electrical overhaul, CP Electrics provides professional domestic electrical services across Birmingham and the West Midlands. We handle all aspects of home electrics with care and precision.

Every installation is carried out to the latest BS 7671 wiring regulations, and we always test our work thoroughly before signing off. Your safety is our top priority.

What We Offer

  • Additional sockets and USB charging points
  • Light switch replacements and upgrades
  • Electrical fault finding and diagnosis
  • Cooker and hob connections
  • Outdoor socket and lighting installation
  • Extractor fan fitting
  • Electric shower installation
  • Smoke and CO alarm fitting

Why Choose Us for Home Electrics?

We understand that having an electrician in your home can be disruptive. That's why we work quickly and efficiently, always keeping our workspace clean and tidy. We explain everything we're doing in plain English, so you're never left wondering what's going on.

With no call-out charge and free estimates, there's no risk in getting in touch. We'll assess your needs, provide an honest quote, and only proceed when you're happy.
