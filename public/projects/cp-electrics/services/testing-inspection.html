Electrical Testing & Inspection Services

Regular electrical testing is essential to ensure your property's wiring is safe and compliant. Whether you're a homeowner, landlord, or business owner, CP Electrics provides comprehensive testing and inspection services across Birmingham and the West Midlands.

EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Report)

An EICR is a detailed assessment of the electrical installations within a property. It identifies any defects, deterioration, or non-compliance with current safety standards. Since June 2020, an EICR has been a legal requirement for all rental properties in England.

  • Legally required for all rental properties
  • Recommended every 5 years for homeowners
  • Required when buying or selling a property
  • Often required by insurance companies

Landlord Electrical Safety Certificates

As a landlord, you are legally required to have the electrical installations in your rental properties inspected and tested at least every 5 years. We provide fast, reliable EICR reports that meet all legal requirements, with remedial work available if any issues are found.

PAT Testing

Portable Appliance Testing (PAT) checks the safety of electrical appliances. While not a strict legal requirement, it's strongly recommended for businesses and landlords as part of their duty of care. We offer competitive rates for PAT testing across all property types.

  • Landlord electrical safety certificates
  • PAT testing for businesses and rental properties
  • Pre-purchase electrical surveys
