About AP Sweepz

Over 15 years of professional road sweeping experience. Trusted by councils, contractors, and businesses across the UK.

Who We Are

AP Sweepz is a professional road sweeper hire company based in Redditch, West Midlands. Founded by Alex Poole, we've grown from a single sweeper operation into one of the Midlands' most trusted sweeping and cleaning service providers.

With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we've built our reputation on three core principles: reliability, competitive pricing, and exceptional service. Our team of experienced operators and modern fleet of Johnston and Stocks sweepers deliver outstanding results on every job.

We understand that every project is different. Whether you need a one-off construction site clean, ongoing highway maintenance, or emergency flood response at 3am, we have the equipment, expertise, and commitment to get the job done right.
