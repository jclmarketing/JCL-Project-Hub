Is Your Garage Roof Putting Your Family at Risk?

Millions of UK garages still have asbestos cement roofing installed before the year 2000. As these roofs age, they deteriorate and release dangerous microscopic fibres into the air around your home.

Health Hazard

Asbestos fibres cause serious lung diseases including mesothelioma and asbestosis

Getting Worse Daily

Weathering and age cause constant deterioration, increasing fibre release

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about asbestos removal and garage roof replacement.

Asbestos-containing materials that are intact and undisturbed are generally low risk. However, if your roof is damaged, weathered, cracked, or you're planning any building work, fibres can be released into the air. These microscopic fibres pose serious health risks including mesothelioma and asbestosis. We strongly recommend professional assessment and removal if your roof is showing signs of deterioration.

Most single and double garage roof replacements are completed in just one day. Our experienced team arrives in the morning, safely removes the old asbestos roof, and installs your brand new, fully insulated roof system — all before the end of the working day.

Yes! We provide nationwide coverage across England and Wales. Our teams are strategically based throughout the country, which means we can offer consistent, competitive local pricing regardless of your location.

Costs vary depending on the size of your garage, the condition of the existing roof, and your choice of replacement materials. We provide free, no-obligation quotes with transparent pricing and no hidden fees. Contact us for an accurate estimate.

All asbestos is removed following strict HSE guidelines. The material is carefully dismantled, double-bagged in certified disposal bags, and transported directly to a licensed waste disposal facility. We provide complete waste transfer documentation for your records.

Every new roof comes with a comprehensive 25-year guarantee covering materials and workmanship. We use only premium-grade roofing systems from leading manufacturers. You'll receive a written guarantee certificate upon completion.
